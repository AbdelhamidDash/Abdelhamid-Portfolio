ANDROID · KOTLIN · GRADUATION PROJECT

CS Track Roadmap & Assessment App

A graduation project built at Modern Academy Maadi. A mobile app that helps Computer Science students identify the right specialization track, understand where they stand, and know exactly what to focus on in their university curriculum — bridging the gap between academic study and real industry requirements.

Modern Academy Maadi  •  Sep 2022 – May 2023

Introduction

Computer Science graduates often leave university with a degree but without a clear picture of which specialization to pursue, where they stand relative to industry expectations, or which subjects in their remaining curriculum actually matter for their chosen path. This graduation project was built to close that gap.

Built at Modern Academy Maadi, the app lets students select a CS specialization track (Android, Web, AI, Data Science, etc.), sit through an adaptive assessment calibrated to that track, and receive a personalized roadmap that maps their university subjects to industry relevance — highlighting exactly what to prioritize and where the knowledge gaps are.

Contributions

  • Designed and implemented the track selection system presenting multiple CS specializations (Android, Web, AI, Data Science) with a clear onboarding flow.
  • Built an adaptive assessment engine — a quiz that evaluates the student's current knowledge level per track across foundational and advanced topics.
  • Engineered the personalized roadmap generator that processes assessment results and produces a prioritized, subject-by-subject learning path tailored to the student's score profile.
  • Developed the gap analysis view, showing a per-topic breakdown of the student's knowledge score with clear labeling of priority gaps versus areas of strength.
  • Implemented progress tracking so students can monitor improvement over time, retake the assessment, and see how their roadmap evolves as they advance.
  • Delivered the full app in Kotlin with MVVM architecture and XML layouts, keeping the codebase clean and presentation-layer logic separate from data handling.

Key Highlights

Track Selection

Students choose from available CS specializations — Android, Web, AI, Data Science — and the entire experience is scoped to that path.

Adaptive Assessment

A quiz evaluates the student's knowledge across track-relevant topics, producing a per-subject score that drives the personalized roadmap.

Personalized Roadmap

Based on assessment results, the app generates a ranked learning path highlighting which university subjects to prioritize next.

Gap Analysis

A topic-by-topic breakdown shows exactly where knowledge gaps exist, so students know precisely what to study rather than guessing.
