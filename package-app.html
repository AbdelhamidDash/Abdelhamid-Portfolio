Introduction

Managing last-mile package delivery across multiple branches is riddled with accountability gaps — packages get lost, deliveries go unconfirmed, and disputes arise with no paper trail to resolve them. PackTrack was built to close these gaps end-to-end.

Built with Flutter, PackTrack is a cross-platform app used by couriers and receivers alike. A courier logs in and is automatically assigned to their branch, scans each package during pick-up, and marks it as delivered on arrival. The receiver at the destination then opens the app and approves the delivery — creating a fully auditable, tamper-proof chain of custody from origin to final acceptance.

Contributions

  • Built a branch-aware authentication system that automatically assigns each courier to their designated branch upon login, restricting visibility to only their own pending packages.
  • Developed a barcode-based package scanning flow for the pick-up step, tying each scan to the courier's account with a timestamped record.
  • Implemented a multi-step delivery status tracker (Pending → Picked Up → In Transit → Delivered) with real-time state updates visible to both courier and receiver.
  • Built the receiver approval screen — receivers must actively confirm receipt, creating a two-party handshake that closes the delivery and prevents disputes.
  • Designed a dispute and issue-reporting flow allowing receivers to flag problems at the point of delivery rather than after the fact.
  • Engineered the entire UI/UX in Flutter, optimised for field use — large tap targets, high-contrast status indicators, and minimal steps to complete a delivery action.

Key Highlights

Scan to Pick Up

Couriers scan each package's barcode to confirm pick-up, creating a timestamped record tied to their account and branch.

Live Status Tracking

Every package moves through a clear multi-step lifecycle — Pending, Picked Up, In Transit, Delivered — with real-time updates.

Receiver Approval

Delivery is only closed when the receiver explicitly confirms receipt in the app — no unilateral marking of packages as delivered.

Branch Assignment

Login automatically scopes each courier to their branch, ensuring they only see and act on packages relevant to their location.
